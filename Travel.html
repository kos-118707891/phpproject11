Travel - Hong Kong

Explore Hong Kong



  1. . Hong Kong is by far the most popular tourist city in the world. Hong Kong has more tourists any other city in the world. About 26 million visitors arrived in 2017 according to Euromonitor, and this is about three million more than to second place Bangkok.


  2. It is rated the top business city in the world. Hong Kong is usually voted the "Best Business City in the World" in the annual readers' poll organized by Business Traveller Asia Pacific magazine. It has the lowest tax rates in Asia. It was rated #1 from 2010 to 2015, though it was #2 in 2016 and 2017. So business conditions are usually more favorable there than any other place in the world.


  3. Most Popular Ferry - The little Star Ferry that carries 19 million people across Victoria Harbour each year.
